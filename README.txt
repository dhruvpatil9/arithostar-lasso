For Compilation and running the lasso loop game:-
Open terminal from codeblocks:
Type: <mingw32-g++ -std=c++11 -o lasso lasso.cpp MovingObject.cpp main.cpp coin.cpp -lsprite -lbgi -lgdi32 -lcomdlg32 -luuid -loleaut32 -lole32> 
> lasso.exe

Short intro about game:-
It is a combination of two games (Lasso BALLSKET & Lasso ARITHOSTAR) of different concepts from which any one can be played by selecting appropriate buttons on the starting screen.
Rules of games are provided in the game interface directly of whichever game you choose (shown in video too).
The demo video shows the features and concepts of both the options of games by clicking on their buttons one by one. 

BALLSKET stands for Basket of Balls.
ARITHO STAR stands for Arithmetically gaining Stars
